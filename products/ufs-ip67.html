UFS IP67

Rugged ultra-fast scanning X-ray imaging for demanding industrial inspection environments.

Technical specs Discuss application

Overview

Industrial-grade.
No cooling required.

UFS IP67 is an industrial linear detector array built for fast scanning applications in harsh or continuous-use environments. IP67 protection, no active cooling, and direct conversion CdTe imaging — engineered for inline inspection workflows where uptime, sensitivity, and long-duration stability matter.

Unlike detector systems that rely on Peltier cooling to manage sensor temperature, UFS IP67 operates without active cooling across its full operating range. The result is a simpler system architecture, lower maintenance overhead, and reliable performance across the temperature range relevant to real industrial environments.

TDI mode operation enables line rates up to 75,000 lines per second — supporting scanning speeds that match the demands of high-throughput production inspection.

Key Features

Engineered for the hardest conditions.

01

IP67 Rugged Enclosure

Fully sealed industrial enclosure rated IP67. Designed for environments with dust, moisture, vibration, and continuous operation demands that would disqualify lab-grade detectors.

02

No Active Cooling

No Peltiers, no cooling circuits. UFS IP67 operates within its full specification across 10–40 °C without active thermal management — reducing system complexity and failure modes.

03

Ultra-Fast TDI Scanning

TDI mode with line rates up to 75,000 lps. Supports scanning speeds from 28 m/min at 100 µm through to 1,700 m/min at 400 µm — matching high-throughput industrial production lines.

04

Direct Conversion CdTe

CdTe direct conversion imaging with 28 TDI stages. Exceptionally high sensitivity, wide energy range (10–360 kV), and 16-bit dynamic range for demanding inspection applications.

05

High Contrast & Sensitivity

Single-photon-sensitive TDI performance. Superior contrast resolution enables reliable detection of small foreign objects, fine structural defects, and low-attenuation features.

06

Gigabit Ethernet

Standard GigE interface. 9–24 V DC power supply range. Straightforward integration into industrial system architectures and control environments.
