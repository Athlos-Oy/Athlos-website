Intraoral Dental

WIOS

Fully wireless, direct conversion intraoral X-ray imaging designed for modern dental workflow.

Discuss WIOS

Overview

Freedom of movement.
No compromise on image.

WIOS is a fully wireless intraoral X-ray imaging sensor built around direct conversion technology. It combines cable-free usability with Athlos imaging expertise — giving dental professionals a more flexible workflow without compromising on positioning accuracy or image performance.

By eliminating the intraoral cable entirely, WIOS removes one of the primary sources of patient discomfort and workflow friction. The sensor connects via Bluetooth, captures directly with the WIOS receiver, and delivers images through a clean integration path into the dental workflow.

WIOS is distributed through regional partners across multiple markets. Dealer inquiries can be directed to Athlos directly.

Key Features

Built for the clinic. Engineered for precision.

01

Fully Wireless Workflow

No intraoral cable — Bluetooth-based wireless link from sensor to receiver. Reduces patient discomfort and simplifies positioning in every clinical setting.

02

Direct Conversion Imaging

Athlos Si-CMOS direct conversion technology converts X-ray photons directly into signal — eliminating the scintillator step and the image degradation that comes with it.

03

Si-CMOS Architecture

Proprietary silicon CMOS readout designed and manufactured by Athlos. Delivers high sensitivity, excellent contrast resolution, and consistent performance.

04

Comfortable Positioning

Holder system designed for accurate and repeatable intraoral placement. Supports proper angulation without relying on the cable for stability.

05

Unique Sensor Design

Compact, purpose-designed form factor that considers both patient anatomy and clinical workflow — a sensor designed from the ground up, not adapted from other platforms.

06

Designed for Dental

Every aspect of WIOS — from sensor geometry to the wireless receiver design to the holder set — is optimised for real-world dental imaging use.

System & Integration

The full WIOS system.

WIOS is supplied as a complete system including the intraoral sensor, the wireless receiver base unit, a full holder set for accurate and comfortable positioning, and integration software. The sensor connects through the Athlos WIOS driver layer into standard dental imaging workflows.
